# Customer Shopping Trends Analysis  
### Retail Customer Behavior & Sales Insights

## Project Overview

Understanding customer shopping behavior is critical for retail and e-commerce businesses to improve conversion, retention, and revenue.

This project analyzes **customer shopping trends** using transactional retail data to uncover insights related to:
- Customer demographics
- Purchase behavior
- Product categories
- Payment preferences
- Discounts, subscriptions, and conversions

The analysis focuses on **business insights**, not just visualizations.

## Key Business Questions Explored

- What does the customer demographic distribution look like?
- Which product categories generate higher average purchase value?
- How do payment methods vary across customers?
- Do discounts and promo codes influence purchase amounts?
- What percentage of customers convert through the funnel?
- Is there a relationship between subscriptions and promo code usage?
- How do ratings and reviews vary across categories?

---

## Key Insights (High-Level)

- Certain product categories show consistently higher average purchase values
- Digital payment methods dominate customer preferences
- Promo code usage does not always correlate with higher purchase amounts
- Subscription users show different shopping behavior compared to non-subscribers
- Customer conversion drops significantly across funnel stages, highlighting optimization opportunities

*(Insights are observational and intended for business understanding.)*
